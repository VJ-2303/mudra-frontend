Asamyukta Hasta

Single-hand gestures forming the foundation of Bharatanatyam expression

28 Mudras
Single Hand
Natyashastra
Showing all 28 mudras
01

Pataka

Flag

Meanings & Usage

Beginning of dance, invocation, forest, river, breeze, sky, clouds, command, blessing.

How to Perform

All five fingers are fully extended and held straight. Fingers are close together. The thumb is straight and aligned alongside the palm.

02

Tripataka

Three-part Flag

Meanings & Usage

Tree, crown, thunderbolt, Indra, power, flame.

How to Perform

The ring finger is bent inward more than halfway toward the palm. The other fingers remain fully extended and held together.

03

Ardhapataka

Half Flag

Meanings & Usage

Leaves, riverbank, horn, writing, precision.

How to Perform

The little finger is bent inward toward the palm. The remaining fingers are fully extended and held together.

04

Kartarimukha

Scissor Face

Meanings & Usage

Cutting, separation, lightning, conflict.

How to Perform

Index and middle fingers are fully extended and separated. Ring and little fingers are bent inward. Thumb supports them.

05

Mayura

Peacock

Meanings & Usage

Beauty, grace, tilak, wiping tears.

How to Perform

The ring finger touches the tip of the thumb. Index, middle, and little fingers are fully extended.

06

Ardhachandra

Half Moon

Meanings & Usage

Moon, blessings, greeting, offering.

How to Perform

All fingers are extended and held together. Thumb is stretched outward away from the palm.

07

Arala

Bent Hand

Meanings & Usage

Drinking nectar or poison, wind, strength.

How to Perform

Index finger is bent inward more than halfway. Remaining fingers are fully extended and held together.

08

Shukatunda

Parrot Beak

Meanings & Usage

Shooting arrows, affection, precision.

How to Perform

Index finger is bent toward the thumb forming a beak shape. Other fingers remain extended.

09

Mushti

Fist

Meanings & Usage

Anger, power, holding weapons.

How to Perform

All fingers including thumb are folded tightly inward forming a fist.

10

Shikhara

Crest

Meanings & Usage

Bow, bell, valor, trident symbol.

How to Perform

Thumb is held upright. Other fingers are bent inward toward the palm.

11

Kapitta

Elephant Apple

Meanings & Usage

Holding flowers, devotion, Lakshmi.

How to Perform

Index finger is bent to touch the base of the thumb. Other fingers are folded inward.

12

Katakamukha

Bracelet Link

Meanings & Usage

Tying garlands, ornaments.

How to Perform

Thumb, index, and middle fingers form a circular shape. Ring and little fingers are extended.

13

Suchi

Needle

Meanings & Usage

Pointing, number one, pain.

How to Perform

Index finger is fully extended upward. Other fingers are bent inward.

14

Chandrakala

Moon Tip

Meanings & Usage

Moon, elegance, curved forms.

How to Perform

Thumb and little finger are extended. Other fingers are bent inward.

15

Padmakosha

Lotus Bud

Meanings & Usage

Flowers, offerings, abundance.

How to Perform

All fingers are gently curved inward forming a bud-like hollow shape.

16

Sarpashirsha

Serpent Head

Meanings & Usage

Snake hood, oil massage, flow.

How to Perform

All fingers are curved together forming a serpent hood shape.

17

Mrigashirsha

Deer Head

Meanings & Usage

Deer, innocence, tenderness.

How to Perform

Index and middle fingers extended together. Ring and little fingers bent inward.

18

Simhamukha

Lion Face

Meanings & Usage

Lion, fierceness, pouring liquids.

How to Perform

Index finger bent inward. Thumb presses against it. Other fingers folded.

19

Kangula

Small Bell

Meanings & Usage

Berries, small fruits, delicacy.

How to Perform

Thumb touches middle finger. Other fingers are bent inward.

20

Alapadma

Open Lotus

Meanings & Usage

Beauty, bloom, elegance.

How to Perform

All fingers are fully spread apart forming an open lotus.

21

Chatura

Clever Hand

Meanings & Usage

Intelligence, counting, subtlety.

How to Perform

Thumb touches ring finger. Other fingers remain extended.

22

Bhramara

Bee

Meanings & Usage

Bee, attraction, Manmatha.

How to Perform

Middle finger bent inward. Other fingers are fully extended.

23

Hamsasya

Swan Beak

Meanings & Usage

Thread, pearl, purity.

How to Perform

Thumb and index finger touch lightly. Other fingers are extended.

24

Hamsapaksha

Swan Wing

Meanings & Usage

Sweeping, waves, air.

How to Perform

All fingers extended together, hand stretched sideways like a wing.

25

Sandamsha

Pincers

Meanings & Usage

Catching, gripping, tension.

How to Perform

Index finger and thumb pinch together. Remaining fingers bent inward.

26

Mukula

Bud

Meanings & Usage

Eating, fruit, offering.

How to Perform

All fingertips including thumb touch together forming a bud.

27

Tamrachuda

Rooster Crest

Meanings & Usage

Rooster, alertness, speed.

How to Perform

Ring finger bent inward. Other fingers are extended and held together.

28

Trishula

Trident

Meanings & Usage

Shiva, fire, protection.

How to Perform

Index, middle, and ring fingers extended upward. Thumb and little finger bent inward.
